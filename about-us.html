Our Story

From a simple idea to India's most trusted educational platform

StudySolutions was founded in 2025 by a group of passionate educators and technology enthusiasts who recognized the challenges students face in accessing quality educational resources. We noticed that many students struggled to find reliable, step-by-step solutions to their NCERT textbook problems.

What started as a small initiative to help local students has grown into a comprehensive platform serving over 2 million students across India. Our journey has been guided by a simple yet powerful mission: to make quality education accessible to every student, regardless of their background or location.

Today, we're proud to be the go-to resource for CBSE students from Class 1 to 12, providing not just solutions but also fostering a deeper understanding of concepts through our detailed explanations, video lessons, and practice materials.
